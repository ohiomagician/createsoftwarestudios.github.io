Bloom Health Pro

by Creative Software Studios

Smart plant care made simple.

Identify plants instantly

Bloom Health Pro allows users to take a photo of a plant, tree, or flower to identify it and receive essential care information, including watering and sunlight needs.

Personalized plant care guidance

Each plant includes tailored recommendations such as how much water it needs, how often to water, and whether it prefers full sun, partial sun, or shade.

Watering reminders & notifications

Users can set watering reminders and receive push notifications so plants are cared for consistently without guesswork.

Weather-aware outdoor alerts

For plants marked as outdoor, Bloom Health Pro monitors local weather conditions and alerts users if temperatures are expected to fall outside a plant’s safe tolerance range.

Track plant health over time

Users can take periodic photos of the same plant to monitor progress, receive updated health feedback, and view changes over time using a visual health graph.

Organize your entire garden

Bloom Health Pro helps users keep all their plants organized in one place, including photos, notes, locations, and care history.
